Ninety Day Plan

How I will make your life easier

First 30 Days

Meet, Learn, and Understand

    In the first 30 days in role my goals will be to:
  • Introduce and establish myself as an immediate point of contact for all technical issues surrounding the role. Build relationships with agency members. Introduce myself to vendors and MNIT to begin working closely with.
  • Work with team to analyze and understand the current flow for inventory, budget, technical issues, vender relations, etc.
  • Start to build data bases around various needs including training, device condition, the device life cycle strategy, and inventory management.
  • Establish routine around, and procedure for, AV health systems checks.
  • Begain to build data bases around budgeting, vendor contracts, future acquisitions.
    • Photo by Wim van 't Einde on Unsplash

    Next 30 Days

    Planning, Strategy, and Implementation

      Having set the groundwork during my first 30 days, the next 30 days I will accomplish:
  • Work with management to perfect workflow and make adjustments from first month.
  • Ensure first 30-day plans have been achieved.
  • Finish development and begin implementation of related policies and program operations. These include but are not limited to a logical touch base process to ensure staff is trained with fully functioning equipment, AV and device health check procedures, inventory and budgeting spreadsheets and procedures, etc.
  • Work with team to identify and resolve gaps in processes and procedures.
    • Photo by Rick J. Brown on Unsplash

    Final 30 Days

    Optimization

      Strategies have been developed and the bugs have begun to be ironed out. The next 30 days will involve:
  • Work with management to continue to perfect workflow and make adjustments to further improve.
  • Ensure first 60 days tasks are completed.
  • Oversee implementation of new procedures and processes, work with team to ensure gaps are resolved. Continue long term planning objectives, annual budget, life cycle implementation, coordination and completion of other agency-wide facilities management projects and initiatives.
  • Develop next 90-day plan based on feedback from management and identified gaps. Continue to expand my usefulness across departments and agencies.
